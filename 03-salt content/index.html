Rose-Gottlieb Method - Virtual Lab

Step 1: Sample Preparation

Weigh 1–2 grams of butter in a flat-bottom flask. Melt it slightly if necessary.

Step 2: Emulsion Breakdown

Add 1 mL of ammonia and 10 mL of ethanol. Swirl until the butter is dissolved.

Step 3: Fat Extraction

Add 25 mL of diethyl ether, shake. Then add 25 mL of petroleum ether and shake again.

Step 4: Repeat Extraction

Repeat the extraction two more times. Combine all ether layers.

Step 5: Solvent Evaporation

Evaporate solvents in a water bath, then dry the flask at 105°C in an oven for 30 minutes.

Step 6: Cooling & Weighing

Cool the flask in a desiccator and weigh it. Calculate fat content.
